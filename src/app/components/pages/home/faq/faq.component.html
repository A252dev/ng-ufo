Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does it work?

A: The service detects the card issuer and sends simultaneous requests for authorization and crediting to your balance in encrypted form, providing information control over transaction statuses.

Q: Can I pay with my phone?

A: Yes. The transaction takes place through the Internet browser of any device, including a smartphone.

Q: Is it safe?

A: Yes. Data is securely protected and encrypted in accordance with the PCI DSS 3.2 Level 1 security certificate.
